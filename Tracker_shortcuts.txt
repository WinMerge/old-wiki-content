''Tracker Shortcuts'' are shorter and nicer URLs to [http://sourceforge.net/tracker/?group_id=13216 Tracker] items from our [http://sourceforge.net/projects/winmerge SF.net project page]:

;Bug:<nowiki>http://winmerge.org/bug/</nowiki>''bug-id''
;Patch:<nowiki>http://winmerge.org/patch/</nowiki>''patch-id''
;Support Request:<nowiki>http://winmerge.org/support/</nowiki>''support-id''
;Feature Request:<nowiki>http://winmerge.org/rfe/</nowiki>''rfe-id''
;Todo Item:<nowiki>http://winmerge.org/todo/</nowiki>''todo-id''
;Tracker Item:<nowiki>http://winmerge.org/tracker/</nowiki>''tracker-id''

You can use this [http://winmerge.org/support/tracker-shortcuts.php online generator] to create the shortcuts.
